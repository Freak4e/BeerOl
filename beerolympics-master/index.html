Beer Pong
Duos

The classic beer game to get the night started right!

Here are the rules for Beer Pong Duos:

1) The Cup Formation is 3-2-1 (6 cups).

2) The length of the game is 10 minutes.

3) Each team is allowed 1 reformation of cups per game.

4) Both players from one team are given a ping pong ball to shoot and may do so simultaneously.

5) During offense, the players can choose between a standard shot and a bounce shot.

6) During offense, the players must keep their elbows behind the table, or the shot will not be counted.

7) Both players from the current team must have taken a shot before the other team can shoot.

8) A standard shot is worth 1 cup.

9) A bounce shot is worth 2 cups.

10) If both shooters score with a normal shot in the same cup, the opposing team loses 3 cups.

11) If the shooters score one bounce and one normal shot, the opposing team loses 3 cups. If they score in the same cup, the opposing team loses 5 cups.

12) If both shooters score a bounce shot the opposing team loses 4 cups. If they score in the same cup, the opposing team loses 6 cups.

13) A bounce shot can be blocked.

14) Illegal blocks cost the defenders one cup.

15) If the shooters win with one cup left, the opposing team is given 2 shots to try to extend the game into overtime.

16) The length of the overtime is 2 minutes.
